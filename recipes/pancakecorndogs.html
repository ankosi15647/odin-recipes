Pancake Corndogs

Description

This is a recipe for the only acceptable kind of corn dogs to consume: breakfast ones! Even though the recipe instructions may say to use regular beef-style sausages, thats not okay. You should be using turkey hot dogs because dead pig flesh is gross :)

Ingredients

  • Pancake Batter
  • Breakfast sausages
  • Oil
  • Syrup
  • Powdered Sugar

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Cook sausage links in skillet
  3. Stick sausages with bambobo skewer or popsicle stick
  4. Make pancake batter
  5. Dip the sausage into the batter
  6. Prepare skillet with oil
  7. Dip hot dog into the pancake batter gently
  8. Rotate until evenly brown, and serve!
